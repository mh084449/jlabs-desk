Privacy Policy – JUMP Desk

Effective date: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

JUMP Desk facilitates communication between our customer service team and sellers via WhatsApp and Google Chat. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Data We Collect

Our bot processes messages, names, and contact information as necessary to relay communications. Media files (images, videos) sent through WhatsApp or Google Chat may also be temporarily processed by our system.

3. How Data is Used

The data is used solely to enable real-time communication between sellers and customer service agents. It helps us provide timely support and ensures messages are delivered correctly.

4. Third-Party Services

Messages and media are processed using n8n cloud, a workflow automation platform. n8n acts as a processor but does not access or share data beyond executing the workflow for JUMP Desk.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties outside of the services required to operate JUMP Desk.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by our bot, including secure transmission and storage practices.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at privacy@jump-labs.com.
